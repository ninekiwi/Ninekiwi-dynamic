Survey & Inspection Form

General Notes

"General Notes" provide essential project guidelines and instructions that are not detailed in specifications or drawings.

  • Ensure compliance with local building codes.
  • Detail material storage guidelines and access restrictions.
  • Communicate all overarching project requirements.

Tenant Protection Inspection

This process ensures tenant safety during renovations in occupied buildings. It involves measures such as:

  • Installing barriers to reduce dust and noise.
  • Ensuring emergency exits remain accessible.
  • Providing tenants timely updates on construction progress.

Survey

Surveys collect site data for planning and executing construction projects efficiently.

  • Topography and boundary mapping.
  • Existing structures and potential risks identification.
  • Ensuring project feasibility based on site conditions.

Survey Questions

  • Any Accidents Today? - Example: "No accidents reported; safety protocols followed."
  • Any Schedule Delays Occur? - Example: "No delays; all tasks on track."
  • Did Weather Cause Any Delays? - Example: "Weather conditions were favorable."
  • Any Visitors on Site? - Example: "Two client representatives visited."
  • Any Areas That Can't Be Worked On? - Example: "North wing under inspection."
  • Any Equipment Rented on Site? - Example: "Crane rented for material lifting."

Project Safety Checklist

  • Exterior: Hazards outside the area? Example: "No hazards identified."
  • Interior: Area free of obstructions? Example: "Work areas cleared and organized."
  • Materials Storage: Proper storage? Example: "Hazardous materials secured."
  • Safety Equipment: Are fire extinguishers accessible? Example: "All extinguishers functional."
  • General Conditions: Site secure? Example: "Perimeter secure; restricted areas marked."
  • Emergency Procedures: Exit routes marked? Example: "All routes marked and visible."
